# Types of jobs
1. Offensive jobs
2. Defensive jobs
### security analyst
- these are often referred to as the digital defenders of the an organisation and sit on the **blue team**
- using their skills , security analyst investigate and piece together potential security incidents , known as **alerts**,to decide if further action is required 
>Example: an employee working in the london office who suddenly logs in from another country
## security engineer 
- security engineers build and maintain the systems and processes that protect an organisation's network and devices
- these are also known as the architects of cyber security
>IDS:intrusion detection system

- this is a specialised defensive role
## penetration tester
- the job is to check how secure a company's systems and software are by legally exploiting them
- the agreement between the company and the penetration tester is known as an **engagement**
- this a broad entry point for jobs
